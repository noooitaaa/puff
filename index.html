radio spugna is an amateur, informal, and precarious radio station, a sound space that comes to life in Rumore, the extra-didactic venue of ISIA in Urbino. The sponge is a porous and relational element: in its highly precarious nature, we deeply recognize ourselves. We believe that radio is a powerful social and cultural device, capable of positioning itself and acting between the public and private spheres. This is why we consider it the best medium to address the issue of precariousness through a horizontal, peer-to-peer approach. The radio offers expanded programming, subject to con- tamination or collaboration: topics range from self-education, books, projects, wi- despread precarity, and music.
AG Fronzoni. Il progetto tra bianco nero is a monographic research about the ita- lian graphic designer AG Fronzoni. Thanks to the access to different archives we were able to collect and photograph part of his work in its original form. The book is divided into two sections: Documenti, a collection of essays and in- terviews around the figure of AGF, and Progetti, containing posters, editorial and branding works, product design, and architecture projects. The materials are presented to best convey the nature of the project, using pho- tographs that either depict the entire object or show it within its original context, marked by a yellow sticker. The final pages of the book, distinguished by the use of yellow paper, feature the text Grafica Italiana dal 1945 a oggi by Carlo Vinti. In these pages, a system has been devised to facilitate the reader’s quick identification of the mentioned names.
