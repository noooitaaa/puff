Qte is a studio dedicated to the development of creative projects, guiding every phase of a project: from concept development and creative research, to design and production, with a critical awareness of tools and methodologies of communication and distribution.


Qte focuses on visual and graphic design, with a particular emphasis on printed matter for publishing and communication; data design and the construction of visual identities and video editing; creative direction and art direction.


Qte works across design, fashion, and art and embraces a porous vision, looking for spaces of contamination and collaboration.

Qte is interested in how content can shape design —and how design, in turn, can shape content.
