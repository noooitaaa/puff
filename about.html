Noa Merlini, Agnese Pozzobon, Martina Valente
Graphic design and Creative development Studios
Milan, Italy
Noa Merlini, Agnese Pozzobon and Martina Valente is a studio run in equal parts by its three members, dedicated to the development of creative projects, guiding every phase of a project: from concept development and creative research, to design and production, with a critical awareness of tools and methodologies of communication and distribution.

Our focus is on visual and graphic design – with a particular emphasis on printed matter – for publishing and communication, data design, visual identities, creative direction and art direction. 

We work across design, fashion, and art and embrace a porous vision, looking for spaces of contamination and collaboration. We're interested in how content can shape design — and how design, in turn, can shape content.
